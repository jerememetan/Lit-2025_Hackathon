Summary: Data Retention
In the case of TechSolutions Inc. v. The People of California, the plaintiff, a cloud storage provider, is being sued for its failure to adequately preserve and produce data pertinent to a criminal investigation. The core of the dispute revolves around the company's data retention policy, which the prosecution alleges was intentionally and prematurely applied to destroy crucial evidence.
